### ğŸ¤ Customer Interaction Tips

#### First Impression Matters

**When you meet the customer:**

1. **Smile and greet professionally**
   - "Good morning! I'm [Your Name] from PharmaFleet."
   - "I have your delivery from [Pharmacy Name]."

2. **Be respectful of their time**
   - "I'll just need a moment to verify your order."
   - Work quickly but carefully

3. **Professional appearance**
   - Wear your PharmaFleet ID badge
   - Clean, professional clothes
   - Well-groomed appearance

#### Communication Best Practices

**DO:**
- âœ… Speak clearly and politely
- âœ… Confirm their name before handing over
- âœ… Explain what you're delivering
- âœ… Ask if they have questions
- âœ… Thank them for choosing PharmaFleet
- âœ… Wish them good health

**DON'T:**
- âŒ Rush or seem impatient
- âŒ Discuss medication details (privacy)
- âŒ Enter their home without permission
- âŒ Handle medications without gloves if required
- âŒ Take tips (company policy)

#### Handling Difficult Situations

**Customer is unhappy about delivery time:**
> "I apologize for the delay. Traffic was heavier than expected. Thank you for your patience."

**Customer has questions about medication:**
> "I understand you have questions, but I'm the delivery driver. Please contact the pharmacy at [number] for medication information. I can only deliver the package."

**Customer wants to change order:**
> "I'm not able to modify orders, but you can call the pharmacy directly at [number] and they'll help you."

**Customer not home but neighbor offers to take it:**
> "I can only leave this with [Customer Name] or someone they specifically authorized. Let me try calling them."

#### Privacy and Confidentiality

**Pharmaceutical deliveries are sensitive:**

- Never discuss a customer's medications with anyone
- Don't read prescription labels out loud in public
- Keep delivery bags sealed until handoff
- If someone asks "what's in the package?" say: "I'm not at liberty to discuss the contents."

### ğŸ¦º Safety Protocols

#### Your Safety Comes First

**Never risk your safety for a delivery.**

#### General Safety Rules

1. **Vehicle Safety**
   - Always wear seatbelt
   - Follow traffic laws
   - Don't use phone while driving
   - Park legally even if it means walking farther

2. **Personal Safety**
   - Let someone know your route
   - Keep phone charged
   - Stay aware of surroundings
   - Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave

3. **COVID-19 / Health Precautions**
   - Keep hand sanitizer in vehicle
   - Wear mask if required by customer or area
   - Maintain distance when possible
   - Wash hands frequently

#### High-Risk Situations

**If you feel unsafe at a delivery location:**

1. **Stay in your vehicle**
   - Call the customer from your car
   - Ask them to come to you
   - If they refuse, mark "Unsafe Location" and leave

2. **Aggressive animals**
   - Don't enter property with loose dogs
   - Call customer to secure pets
   - Use "Unsafe Location" if needed

3. **Suspicious activity**
   - Don't enter areas with active crime
   - Report to support immediately
   - Your safety is more important than any delivery

#### Emergency Procedures

**If you're in danger:**

1. **Leave immediately** - don't worry about the delivery
2. **Call emergency services** (999 in Kuwait)
3. **Call PharmaFleet emergency line**
4. **Find a safe location** (police station, busy store)
5. **Report incident** to support once safe

**If you're in an accident:**

1. Check for injuries
2. Call emergency services if needed
3. Call support to report and get orders reassigned
4. Take photos of damage
5. Follow company accident protocol
