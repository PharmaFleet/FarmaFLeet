## Collecting Payments

### Cash on Delivery (COD)

1. Collect cash from customer
2. Confirm the amount in the app
3. Tap **Confirm Collection**

### Knet/MyFatoora

1. Customer pays electronically
2. Select payment method in app
3. Confirm payment received

---

## Proof of Delivery

When completing a delivery:

### Signature Option

1. Tap **Signature**
2. Customer signs on screen
3. Tap **Done**

### Photo Option

1. Tap **Photo**
2. Take a picture of the delivered package
3. Tap **Confirm**

---

## Rejecting an Order

If you cannot complete a delivery:

1. Tap the order
2. Tap **Cannot Deliver**
3. Enter the reason:
   - Customer not available
   - Wrong address
   - Customer refused
   - Other (specify)
4. Tap **Submit**

The order returns to the manager for reassignment.
